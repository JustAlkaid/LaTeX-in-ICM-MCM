\noindent
Typical Values (Samsung INR21700 30T 3Ah Li-ion Battery):
\begin{itemize}
    \item Heat capacity: $C = 160 \mathrm{J/K}$
    \item Convective heat transfer coefficient: $h = 5 \mathrm{W}/(\mathrm{m}^2 \cdot \mathrm{K})$
    \item Smartphone surface area: $A = 200 \mathrm{cm}^2$
    \item Processor heat fraction: $\eta = 0.5$
    \item Other heat generation: $Q_{other} = 0.8 \mathrm{W}$
    \item Shutdown voltage: $V_{min} = 3.2 \mathrm{V}$ (device stops regardless of SOC)
    \item Thermal shutdown temperature: $T_{max} = 50 \mathrm{^\circ C}$ (protection limit)
\end{itemize}
