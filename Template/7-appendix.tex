\noindent
Typical Values (Samsung INR21700 30T 3Ah Li-ion Battery):
\begin{itemize}
    \item Heat capacity: $C = 160$ J/K
    \item Convective heat transfer coefficient: $h = 5$ W/(m$^2 \cdot$K)
    \item Smartphone surface area: $A = 200$ cm$^2$
    \item Processor heat fraction: $\eta = 0.5$
    \item Other heat generation: $Q_{other} = 0.8$ W
    \item Shutdown voltage: $V_{min} = 3.2$ V (device stops regardless of SoC)
    \item Thermal shutdown temperature: $T_{max} = 50$ C (protection limit)
\end{itemize}
